# Rocket Avionics & Flight Systems

## Overview
Designed and integrated onboard avionics systems for amateur rockets to collect and analyze in-flight performance data. The project focused on sensor integration, flight stability analysis, and recovery system validation under real launch conditions.

---

## Objectives
- Collect onboard flight data during launch and descent
- Evaluate rocket stability and flight behavior
- Validate simulation predictions using real-world launch data
- Develop reliable avionics systems capable of operating under high acceleration and vibration conditions

---

## Systems & Components
- Accelerometers
- Altimeters
- Onboard cameras
- Arduino / Raspberry Pi systems
- Recovery systems
- OpenRocket flight simulations

---

## Engineering Work
- Integrated and soldered onboard avionics hardware
- Designed rocket structures and recovery configurations
- Analyzed flight stability and launch behavior using OpenRocket
- Iterated recovery and avionics configurations through testing and flight evaluation
